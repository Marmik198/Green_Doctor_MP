Plant diseases detection

This is a deployment of a deep learning model (PyTorch) for Classification of 14 crop species. The model leverages the Microsoft's ResNet50 architecture and applies the ImageNet weights, extra training is done through transfer learning, hence it can detect and classify crop leaves into 38 different classes (crop species and status).

About PlantVillage Dataset

In order to ease plant disease diagnosis, the PlantVillage project began collecting and later opensourced images of crops for both healthy and diseased plant. The PlantVillage dataset contains images of leaves corresponding to 14 crop species: Apple, Blueberry, Cherry, Grape, Orange, Peach, Bell Pepper, Potato, Raspberry, Soybean, Squash, Strawberry, and Tomato. The images are divided into 38 classes of crop disease pairs
